* Discrete data (command type): Ignore & warn
* Analog data (duration, pos, vel): Clip & warn

### Extension head

A(right): screw rack

B(left): dump


### I2C bus

DRV8830 (1/3)

* OUT: Train motor
* Addr[3:0]: 0000 (A0: L, A1: L)

DRV8830 (2/3)

* OUT: Orientor motor
* Addr[3:0]: 0001 (A0: open, A1: L)

DRV8830 (3/3)

* OUT: Extension motor (screw)
* Addr[3:0]: 0010 (A0: H, A1: L)
